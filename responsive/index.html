My Story

I was born in Burlington Vermont on Feburary 27th, 2003. After living in Burlington for about two years my family moved to the neighboring town, Essex. From there I grew up in a quant condo, attending Essex Elementary, Essex Middle, and Essex High School. I have made many life long friends in my home town as I spent most of my life there. After attending one year at Essex High School I started looking for better education. From touring ten boarding schools I narrowed my choice down to one that fit me best, Avon Old Farms in Avon Connecticut. Since starting Avon I have aquired many new skills. At AOF I became "Captain Avon", a superior leadership role at the school. With this role I have learned how to lead and excite my fellow peers during times of competition. I love my life and everything I have learned from my experiences.
